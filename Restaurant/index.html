Sarah's Kitchen has been family owned and operated since 1989. We use local farmer's products whenever possible to provide fresh, sustainable dishes. Chef Sarah meticulously pairs the ingredients to offer the best dining experience possible. Often, specials will be comprised of produce from the local Florida Farmer's Market. Sarah's Kitchen prides itself on keeping its ingredients and collaborations local. From the gracious service to the charming atmosphere, Sarah's certainly strives to provide a spectacular overall dining experience. Come in for a delicious meal topped off with dessert from neighbor, Sarah's Homemade Candy Company, or a special event featuring Dessert on Main from down the street.
